Welcome to the Grand Stage

Maestro.js is not merely a memory game, but a test of your musical prowess. Here's how to partake in this melodious challenge:

  • The Maestro, your musical counterpart, commences the melody with a solitary note. Replicate this note to begin.
  • Each round, Maestro will additional notes to the melody. Retain and repeat the evolving melody to advance.
  • Play the notes by gracefully sounding the keys with your mouse, or by skillfully strumming the corresponding keys on your keyboard.
  • Strive to prolong the melody as long as your memory permits!
